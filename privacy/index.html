Loura's Oasis — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The short version: Loura's Oasis collects nothing.

  • No data collection. The game does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, usage data, or device data. There are no analytics services, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code that phones home.
  • Fully offline. The game makes no network requests. Your progress (levels, stars, coins, settings) is stored only on your device, inside the app's own container, and is removed when you delete the app.
  • No account. There is nothing to sign up for and nothing to log in to.
  • No tracking. The app contains no trackers and declares NSPrivacyTracking = false in its Apple privacy manifest.
  • Ratings. If you choose to rate the game, the standard Apple rating sheet handles everything; we never see who rated or what you tapped.
  • Children. The game is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

  • بلا تتبّع. لا تحتوي اللعبة على أي متتبعات.
